An interactive web-based configurator that allows users to customize products in real-time. The interface enables users to visualize and customize products with immediate visual feedback.

Users can adjust various product parameters, seeing their changes reflected instantly. The configurator provides real-time 3D previews, allowing users to see exactly how their customizations will look.


The design guides users through a seamless customization process from selection to final product visualization.
